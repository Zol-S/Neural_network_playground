Text learning capabilities of LSTM
Paste some text into the training textbox or click either Sample text buttons to populate the training text textbox. Adjust the hyperparameters of the neural network (input word counter, dimension of embedding, number of LSTM cells and number of epochs), then click train button to start the training of the neural network. After the training is finished, type the input words to the prediction input field and click predict button. The number of input words should match with the number of input word counter.
